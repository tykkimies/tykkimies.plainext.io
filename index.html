Alexa Account Linking
This page should be opened by the Alexa app during skill setup, not directly. If you're testing, open it from the Alexa app by enabling the skill and tapping "Link Account".

Link your plAInext server

Paste your Command URL below. You can find it on the Voice Setup page inside your plAInext instance.

1
Sign in to your plAInext instance and open Voice → Alexa Setup.
2
Copy the Command URL shown on that page.
3
Paste it below and tap Link Account.

Includes your personal token — keep it private.
